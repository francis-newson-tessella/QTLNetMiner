The Wheat (Triticum aestivum) information network is TGACv1.0 and contains:

    Datasets used for integration

    • Gene
      • Triticum_aestivum.IWGSP1.21.gff3 from Ensembl
    • Protein

    • Protein-Domain
      • BioMart InterPro Protein Domains
      • BioMart PFam Protein Domains
    • Gene Ontology
      • BioMart GO
    • References
      • Arabidopsis (TAIR) see Release notes for Arabidopsis
      • Plants (UniProt) - Information of well observed plant organisms excluding Arabidopsis
        UniProt: taxonomy:33090 NOT taxonomy:3702 AND reviewed:yes
    • Homology
      • BioMart Homologs Wheat - Arabidopsis
      • BioMart Homologs Wheat - Barley
      • Decypher
    • Ontologies
      • gene_ontology.1_2.obo
      • interpro2go
      • pfam2go
      • ec2go
